My Roles

Host and Busser

My Responsibilities

Host:

  • Answered phone calls, made reservations, and folded and cleaned menus
  • Maintained a clean, organized, and fully stocked station
  • Made use of downtime by making sure others were caught up
  • Coordinated with servers and table layout to ensure guests were distributed evenly and sat in a timely manner
  • Interacted with the guests during the walk to the table and accommodated any request they might have had
  • Kept guests company if they were waiting up front on their way out

Busser:

  • Made sure that tables were cleared, cleaned, and set for the next party shortly after the guests left
  • Helped servers meet their table's needs and keep guests satisfied
  • Regularly checked on the dining room for anything that in need of cleaning or guests needing assistance
  • Communicated with the hosts so everyone had the latest updates on the dining room
Dates Employed

King's Fish House, Tempe AZ Dec 2019 - Mar 2020

About King's Fish House

We’ve been in restaurants a little while – three generations, to be precise. A long time ago, we embarked on a road trip in search of the ultimate seafood experience. We traveled down highways and byways, pulled up a seat, put on bibs and soaked up the seafood experience: the no-name dishes with unforgettable flavor, the fresh-caught oysters and clams piled high, the hand-lettered specials on ancient chalkboards and the savory simmer of every combination of spices imaginable. The pleasure of preparing and sharing seafood was simple and genuine and it brought out the best in everyone. We felt as if we’d been welcomed into somebody’s home when we pulled up a chair. That’s what King’s Fish House is all about and what we bring to you.
